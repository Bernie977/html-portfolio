About Me

I was born in Ireland and initially trained as an accountant/auditor (ACCA). After qualifying, I left this profession to work in Africa. After many years of experience there, I moved to Cambodia where I married my Khmer husband and we have settled here since then (now over 30 years.

In Cambodia my experience has been implementing development projects with NGOs in many remote rural provinces. The work focuses mainly on livelihood/food security, WASH and Disaster Risk Reduction (DRR) and Climate Change Adaptation (CCA).

After implementing projects for many years, I "semi" retired and now work only on short-term consultancies. These include conducting evaluations, surveys, needs assessments as well as supporting capacity building and organizational development for local NGOs.
